Quick Start Guide

1

Install SnapSum

Click the download button above or visit the Chrome Web Store / Edge Add-ons store.

2

Pin the Extension

Click the puzzle piece icon in your browser toolbar and pin SnapSum for easy access.

3

Start Recording

Click the SnapSum icon → Select what to record → Click Record. That's it!

4

Get AI Subtitles

After recording, AI automatically transcribes and generates subtitles. Translate to any language with one click.
